##Amuna awiri akugwira ntchito yomanga usiku pansi pa kuwala.
##Anyamata asanu atayima pamphepete mwa nyanja ndi mlatho patali akumwetulira pamene mnyamata wakutsogolo, atavala chovala chofiira ndi nsapato za buluu, akutsamira kumbuyo kuti aponye chinachake.
##Sitolo kapena nyumba yokhala ndi mwamuna ndi mkazi kutsogolo .
##Mwamuna wovala t-shirt yakuda ndi magalasi akuyang'ana chithunzi kuntchito kwake .
##Mnyamata yemwe ali ndi visor akusewera mpira ndi mleme wake mumsewu .
##Tsitsi la mtsikana limakwiririka pamene akukwera masitepe pa skateboard .
##Mnyamata wokhazikika bwino amaima ndi phazi limodzi pafupi ndi gombe loyera la nyanja.
##Atsikana awiri, m’modzi wovala sweti yofiyira ndipo wina wovala sweti yabuluu akucheza ndi bambo wadazi wokhala m’galimoto ndipo wanyamula timatumba tating’ono ta njere.
##Gulu la ana aang’ono limayang’ana gulu la ana okulirapo akuseŵera mpira mumsewu wa mumzinda .
##Mwana wamng'ono wa tsitsi loderapo pamapewa, atavala malaya achikasu ndi thalauza lapinki, akuyenda akudya zokondwa kuchokera m'thumba la zipi.
##Galu woyera m’chipale chofeŵa amanyamula botolo lapulasitiki la Mountain Dew m’kamwa mwake .
##Mnyamata akusangalala kwambiri ndi masewera a mpira.
