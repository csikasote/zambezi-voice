##Mbwabede mutolo abulo bwakwe, Mutolo ulanjizya ijanza lyakwe mumbede, Pele cilamukataazya kuliboozya kumulomo wakwe,
##Mutolo musongo mumeso aakwe mwini Kwiinda bantu bali musanu ababili bezi kuvuwa kabotu,
##Uukazyanya munkondo iitali yakwe Uli mbuli uujata mubwa kumatwi uuinda. Mbuli sicinsondo uuwaala-waala zisisi amivwi alufu,
##Mbwabede muntu uuena mweenzinyina Akwaamba kuti, Ndasekana buyo. Na taakwe inkuni, mulilo ulazima;
##Na taakwe sikuvwiya, inkondo ilamana. Mbuli masizi kumakala aayaka, ambuli inkuni kumulilo, Mbwabede sikukazyanya kukubusya inkondo.
##Majwi aasikuvwiya ali mbuli zilyo zinono, Alanjila mukati kabula. Mbuli cibelesyo cabulongo cizilulwidwe ansiliva,
##Mbwiibede milomo myuuba antoomwe amoyo mubi. Uusula akuupaupa amilomo Akusisa lweeno mumoyo wakwe,
##Nekuba kuti ulaamba cabuuya, utamusyomi, Nkaambo muli zisesemyo zili musanu azibili mumoyo wakwe;
##Nekuba kuti lusulo lwakwe lulisisidwe alweeno, Bubi bwakwe bulayubununwa mumbungano yabantu,
##Uusya cilindi ulawida mulincico; Uukunkulisya ibbwe, lilamupilukila. Lulimi lubeja lulisulide abo mbolupwayide; Amulomo muuba ulaleta lunyonyooko.
##Utalikankaizizyi zyajunza, Nkaambo tozi na buzuba bulaleta nzi. Muntu umbi akutembaule, mulomo wako omwini pe;
##Mweenzu, kutali kumilomo yako, Ibbwe ndidemu, awalo musee ulalema, Pele bukali bwamufubafuba mbulemu kwiinda zyoonse.
##Bukali bulayoosya, lunyemo lulakataazya; Pele ibbivwe, ngwani uukonzya kwiima kulindilyo?
##Kutapatila kaanzambwene Nkubotu kwiinda luyando lusisidwe. Kuyasa kwamuzwalani kulasyomeka,
##Pele imyonto zyasinkondonyoko zilavula. Muntu uukutide ulasesemwa buci, Pele kulisinzala zintu zilula zyoonse nzinono,
##Mbuli kayuni kaswekede kuciteente cako, Mbwabede muntu uuswekede kuzwa kulinguwe,
##Mafuta atununkilizyo zilabotezya moyo; Mbulubede lulayo lwamweenzinyoko uuyandika.
##Mweenzinyoko, amweenzinyina uso, utabalekelezyi; Alimwi utayi kuŋanda yamunyoko mubuzuba bwamapenzi.
##Mweenzinyoko uuli kufwaafwi mubotu Kwiinda munyoko uuli kule. Songwaala, mwanaangu, ukondezye moyo wangu,
##Kuti mbe aluvwiilo kuliyooyo uundisampaula. Muntu uucenjede, abona mapenzi, ulayuba,
##Pele bataiyide balaindilila aboobo balapenga, Nyanzya muntu cikobela cakwe na wasyomezya kuliyila mweenzu;
##Umujate na walisyomezya mumwanakazi muzwakule. Muntu uufuma akulongezya mweenzinyina ajwi ipati, Cilaambwa kuti ulatuka buyo,
##Mbuli mandomondomo mubuzuba bwamvula,Mbwabede mwanakazi sikukazyanya; Uumukasya ulakasya muuwo,
##Uli mbuli mafuta mumaanza aakwe. Lubulo lulanona lubulonyina, Mbubonya obo muntu ulanona busyu bwamweenzinyina,
##Uubamba mukuyu ulalya micelo yawo, Ayooyo uubamba simalelaakwe ulalemekwa, Mbuli busyu mbububoneka mumaanzi,
##Mbubonya obo moyo wamuntu mbuuboneka kumuntunyina. Kwacimvumwe alufu tazikuti;
##Aalo meso aamuntu taakuti, Cuumpilo ncansiliva, ceengelo ncangolida, Pele muntu ulasunkwa kumpuwo yakwe,
##Nekuba kuti watwa mufubafuba muncili amunsi antoomwe amaila, Bufubafuba bwakwe tabumani pe,
##Ncobeni uleelede kuziba imbelele zyako mbozibede, Akubika moyo wako kumatanga aako;
##Nkaambo imbono tazikalilili lyoonse, Amunsini tuuli kumazyalani oonse. Bwizu bwamana kuligwa, mweemvwe ulamena,
##Azisyu zyakuzilundu zyacelwa, Tubelele tulakupa zyakusama, Ampongo zilaleta muulo wamuunda;
##Malili aampongo alakuvulila, Alaba zilyo zyabasiŋanda bako. Akulela bana bako basimbi,
##Basizibi balacija nekuba kuti taakwe uubatanda, Pele baluleme balaima mbuli mulavu.
##Na cisi cabisya, cilijisi beendelezi banji, Pele mumaanza aabantu basongo abasiluzibo cilakalilila,
##Mutengwana uupenzya mucetenyina Uli mbuli imvula yamucobocobo litapi zilyo.
##Basowa mulao balatembaula basizibi, Pele basikubamba mulao balakazyanya abo.
##Bantu babi tabamvwisyi kubeteka, Pele basikuyandaula Jehova balamvwisya.
##Muntu mutengwana uuenda cakululama. Mubotu kwiinda muntu muvubi uupilingene.
##Uubamba mulao mwana musongo, Pele uuenda abasindya ulausya wisi insoni.
##Uuvuzya imbono zyakwe kukujana impindu zyazikwelete. Ulaziyobweda sikufwida bacete luzyalo.
##Na muntu wasinka matwi aakwe kukumvwa mulao, Acikombyo cakwe cilaba cisesemyo.
##Uuongelezya baluleme kubatola muzibi, Ulawida mumonya mucilindi ncaakasya, Pele baluleme balajana lukono lubotu,
##Muntu muvubi musongo mumeso aakwe mwini, Pele mutengwana uucenjede ulamvwisya mbwabede.
##Na baluleme bazunda, bulemu bulavula, Pele na basizibi babuka, bantu balayuba, Uusisa milandu yakwe takooyooba acoolwe pe,
