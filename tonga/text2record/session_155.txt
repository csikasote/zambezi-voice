##mwalumi muna Asia ulakusambala cakulya cinona a tefule lyakwe a misika uzwide bantu.
##mwana musankwa ukkede a mubili wa namutekenya wa mota kalakugwasya kunyona mu dulaivilo.
##muaintu usamide cisani ca myengo ya cikambwe a isiya ulakutondezya lawaile lyakwe ku mwalumi usamide ma gogo a cisani cilamyengo wa mungungawaji alimwi a cikambwe a kati ka nzila.
##mukaintu usamide ngowani mpati yamubalo wa nkwiki-nkwiki a kutuba ulikkedemunsi a bamalumi banji basamide zi sani zya cikambwe a kutuba a ntambo mumusana zya mungungawaji kumbele a ngoma zinji zibikidwe a ngowani mbili isamide mukaintu.
##basimusobano muzisani zyabo kaba ezezya kasobano, elyo mwalumi wa susu lisiya a cisani cituba ulikkede a manela.

##mwana musankwa wadubula ntobolo yakusonya kainwi munsi a dangamwa.
##mukaintu usamide ngowani ya nkwiki-nkwiki ulakulanga a cakulya cibikidwe a ma tray ma pati mumbali a nzila.
##muntu ulakugusya cintu mu bbeke a kasanza ka kujikila.
##kuli zifwanikiso zinji-anji mu dolopo omuno zilembedwe mu mulaka umbi.
##bana Asia banji-anji balaenda-enda mu lubazu lwa dolopo bantu banji nkoba baula katundu wabo.
##mwalubi usamide mulembo usalala ulakwenda kuya ku makankala a tuba a salala malamfu kapati.
##mwalumi utasamide cisani uli a chibbudula ci tuba ujisi kantu kakumya ka neti a tu neti mu kantu ka siya.
##bantu banji-anji balakkoma kabalakuzabusigwa mu bwato.
##mwana usamide zisani zya cikambwe ulakusobana a nkoloko njasamide.

##elyo mwana musankwa ka lobola baswi a magineti, baushi balakusola kujata baswi ba puta balakuyamba mu manzi.
##bantu bobilo balikkede mu nzila ya mabwe ibambidwe kabotu ilengene a manzi.
##mwalumi ulikkede a chuuno ca kumusasa munsi a bbwe kalangide ku dambwa lya manzi.
##bakaintu botatwe basamide ma delesi a misyobo i yaka kabotu-kabotu a ntambala alimwi a mwalumi alimwi a mwana a kati kabo balakwenda mu caanda.
##mukaintu ujisi muchila wa bbiza kasamide a cibbudula ca jiini a cisani ca kujulu cisiya ukkede a mubili wa mwalumi usamide cibbudula a cisani ci tuba.
##musimbi nakalindu usamide cisani cikambwe wa nyazabili ulinvwi mu ncikini.
##bantu bobilo balainvwi anze mumbali a mupanda wa nsefa mu busena bwakumbali a dolopo.
##mwalumi a mukaintu balakumbatana a kumyontana munsi a basikwimba balakwimba.
##kuli basimbi bobilo balikunembo a kabunga kabantu mpona bajisi tu bbeke twakubbala a gwezyo twa ma bbuku.
##mulombwana ulinvwi a cisamu kalakunuw mu nkapu.
##mukanitu usamide cisani ci siya-siya ulakuma kalizyo ka moya kumbele a bantu a mwalumi usamide cituba ulakuma harmonica alimwi aumbi uma bbanjo.
##mubwa u siya-siya unyanide ulakusotoka a musunta wa bwizu munzila ili a matombe.
##mwalumi usamide zisani zyakuyambya zya mukuba uli a kusotoka kuzya a bbwe damfu kuya mu menda.
##mwana mukubusyi ulakusola kuzabuka kalonga basamkwa bambi bobilo ka ba musekelela.
##mwalumi usamide cisani cituba ulinvwi a microphone ku mbele a mulemboa usalala.
##mubwa usiya siya a mabala a tuba alimwi a mubwa utuba a kusiya balakusobana a ka neti a ng'anda.
##mwalumi usamide jansi lya cikambwe ulakubamba zintu mu pulasitiki ituba mumbali a nzila.
##mukaintu usamide ma gogo ulakumweta kumbele a lwizi lya ocean.
##mulombwana ali a bbili pati usamide cisani ca cikambwe a magogo a siya kuti zwii ulakuma bbanjo mu nzila.
