##Matalikilo: milimo yatusimpi
##Ntootu tusimpi twa-Solomoni mwana wa-Davida mwami wa-Israyeli: Kuti bantu bazibe busongo alulayo,
##Baswiilile majwi aakumvwisya, Batambule lulayo lwakucenjeia, Bululami, ambeta, aboololoki;
##Kuti babombumyoyo bapegwe maanu, Abalombe bajane luzibo alumvwisyo;
##Kuti musongo aswiilile akuliyungizizya lwiiyo, Asimaanu alijanine lulayo; Bantu bamvwisye tusimpi azikozyano,
##Majwi aabasongo atulabi twabo. Lucenjezyo lwanzila zibi. Kuyoowa Jehova matalikilo aaluzibo,
##Pele bafubafuba balasampaula busongo alulayo. Swiilila, mwanaangu, lulayo lwauso,
##Alimwi utataalazyi mulao wabanyoko, Nkaambo zila ba ingala kumutwe wako, Aluwenje munsingo yako.
##Mwanaangu, na basizibi bakoongelezya, Utazumini, Na baamba kuti, Katuya ayebo, tulindile bulowa,
##Tujane bantu batakwe mulandu, Tubamene baciputa mbuli kwacimvumwe, Tubamenenene mbuli abo baselukila mukabanda;
##Lino tuyoojana zintu ziyandika, Akuzuzya maanda esu imbono; Waala ciselo cako munkamu yesu,
##Toonse tube acikwama comwe buyo, Mwanaangu, uteendi abo munzila, Kasya maulu aako kunzila zyabo;
##Nkaambo maulu aabo alacijaanina bubi, Balafwambaanina kutila bulowa.
##Ncobeni taakwe milimo kuteya kakole Mumeso aamuyuni, Pele bantu aba balalindila bulowa bwabo beni, Basi jate buumi bwabo beni.
##Mbuzibede inzila zyaboonse bajana impindu yabubi; Ilanyanzya buumi bwabasikwiivuba,
##Busongo mbobucenjezya bantu. Busongo bulaita mumigwagwa, Buloongolola mumasambalilo.
##Atala amalambo bulaita, Akumanjililo aamilyango yamunzi bulaamba kuti,
##Inywe nomutezi, mulaleka lili kuyandisya kutaziba? Anywebo nobasikasampusampu, kusampaula kulaleka lili kumubotezya?
##Nobafubafuba mulaleka lili kusula luzibo? Amucenjele kutapatila kwangu. Njoomutilila muuya wangu,
##Njoomuzibya majwi aangu. Ndakaita, pele mwakakaka; Ndakatandabika maanza aangu, pele teemwakaswiilila;
##Mwakasampaula lulayo lwangu loonse, Akutabika myoyo yanu kukucenjezya kwangu.
##Nkaambo kako ambebo njooseka kumapenzi aanu, Njoomusampaula kuciindi cakuyoowa kwanu,
##Buyoofu nibuyoomusikila mbuli iguwo, Amapenzi aanu mbuli kambizi, Nimuyoopenga akuciswa mumyoyo yanu.
##Lino bayoondiita, pele nsikooyoovuwa; Bayoondiyandaula, pele tabakooyoondijana.
##Bakasula luzibo, Teebakasala kuyoowa Jehova; Bakakaka kuswiilila lulayo lwangu, Bakasampaula kucenjezya kwangu;
##Nkaambo kako bayoolya micelo yanzila yabo, Bayookuta makanze aabo. Nkaambo batezi kuleyauka kwabo kulabajaya,
##Akuliiba kwabafubafuba kulabanyonyoona, Pele abo baswiilila ndime bayookala kabotu, Bayooba aluumuno cakutayoowa bubi.
