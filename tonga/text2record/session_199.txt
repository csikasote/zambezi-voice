##Utabbidi mucete, nkaambo mutengwana, Nekuba kuuma bapengede mumulyango,
##Nkaambo Jehova uyoobeteka makani nabo. Akunyanzya basikubanyanzya buumi.
##Utatangi buzwalani amuntu mukati, Nekuba kweenda amuntu silunyemo, Kuti utaiyi inzila zyakwe,
##Akulyaanga mukakole. Utabi umwi wabaabo bapa zitondezyo, Basyomezya kuliyila basizikoloto.
##Na wabula cakuliya, Sa ncibotu kunyanzigwa bulo bwako? Utasunzululi munyinza wakale
##Ngobakabika bamauso, Sa ulibwene muntu uusungwaala mumilimo yakwe?
##Oyo muntu ulaima kunembo lyabami, Takooyooima kunembo lyabantu buyo pe.
##Na wakala kukulya amweendelezi, Yeeya kabotu cilyo ncowabikilwa;
##Lino utaingani, Na uli sindya; Utasukami zilyo zyakwe zilweela, Nkaambo nzilyo zyalweeno.
##Utalikataazyi kuti ube muvubi; Leka mubusongo bwako, Imbono zyabonwa buyo, zilamana ndilyonya,
##Zilalicitila mababa, Zilauluka mbuli sikwaze uuya kujulu, Utalyi zilyo zyasyacivulamwangu;
##Utasukami zilyo zyakwe zilweela; Nkaambo ulayeeya-yeeya mumoyo wakwe, Ulaamba kuti, Kolya akunywa,
##Nekubaboobo moyo wakwe tuuli ayebo. Minsuma njowalya ulailuka, Alimwi ulasowa buyo majwi aako mabotu,
##Utaambi mumatwi aamufubafuba, Nkaambo ulasampaula busongo bwamajwi aako,
##Utasunzululi munyinza wakale, Nekuba kunjila mumyuunda yabamucaala, Nkaambo Munununi wabo ngusinguzu,
##Ulabeteka makani aabo kulinduwe, Bika moyo wako kululayo, Akucenjeka kutwi kwako kumajwi aaluzibo,
##Utakaki kukoma mwana; Na wamuuma amusako, takoofwa pe; Na wamuuma amusako,
##Ulavuna buumi bwakwe kulufu. Mwanaangu, na moyo wako uli abusongo, Moyo wangu ambebo ulakondwa.
##Njookondwa mukati kansa zyangu, Na milomo yako ilaamba ziluleme. Moyo wako utafwidi basizibi ibbivwe,
##Pele kaka tila kukuyoowa Jehova buzuba boonse, Nkaambo mamanino ayooboneka,
##Nkabela bulangizi bwako tabukooyooloba, Swiilila, mwanaangu, ujate busongo;
##Enzya moyo wako munzila, Utakali akati kabacakolwa, Nanka akati kamadyanyama,
##Nkaambo mukolwi asindya bayoosikilwa bucete, Alimwi butolo bulasamika muntu madeludelu.
##Swiilila uso iwakakuzyala, Alimwi utasampauli banyoko na bacembaala. Koula lusinizyo, utaluulisyi pe;
##Koula busongo alulayo abupampu. Wisi mululami ulasekelela loko; Uuzyala musongo ulajana kukondwa mulinguwe,
##Uso abanyoko basekelele; lwakakuzyala akondwe. Mwanaangu, undipe moyo wako;
##Meso aako alange inzila zyangu, Nkaambo mwanakazi muvuule ncilindi cilamfu; Mwanakazi uuzwakule mugodi uusyanikide,
##Ulalindila mbuli sikubba, Ulavuzya batasyomeki akati kabantu. Ngwani uuli abuusu? Ngwani uuli amapenzi?
##Ngwani uuli ankondo? Ngwani uuli akutongauka? Ngwani uuli amibunda katakwe kaambo?
##Ngwani uuli ameso aasalala pyu? Mbembabo bakala ciindi cilamfu kukunywa,
##Baya kukuyandaula waini uuvwelene, Utalangi waini ciindi naasalala, Naanjoloma munkomeki,
##Naatelemuka kabotu. Kumamanino ulaluma mbuli inzoka, Ulazapula mbuli cipile.
##Meso aako alabona zintu ziyoosya, Amoyo wako ulaamba makani aapilingene.
##Ulaba mbuli muntu uulala mukati kalwizi, Na mbuli muntu uulala akasamu kandembela.
##Ulati, Bakanduuma pele teendakaciswa, Bakandiyasa pele teendakamvwa, Nsinsimuke lili?
##Nkayandaule cakunywa alimwi. Utafwidi bantu babi ibbivwe; Utasukami kuba abo;
##Nkaambo myoyo yabo ilayeeya lunyonyongano, Amilomo yabo ilaamba zitaluleme.
##Iŋanda ilayasigwa busongo, Ilaimikwa amaanu; Kuluzibo twaanda twayo tulazuzigwa
##Imbono zyoonse ziyandika zibotezya. Muntu musongo mubotu kwiinda sintaze;
##Luzibo ndubotu kwiinda inguzu; Nkaambo busongo mbobugwasya kucita inkondo.
##Alimwi muli basinkuta banji molwanwa lufutuko. Busongo tabweenzeki kumufubafuba;
##Tajuli mulomo wakwe kumulyango. Uukanza kucita bubi Ulaambwa kuti ngusilupyopyongano.
##Kuyeeya zipilingene nzibi; Sikasampusampu ulasesemya bantu, Na wawizuka mubuzuba bwamapenzi,
##Inguzu zyako zyaceya, Vuna abo basi tolwe kulufu; Futula abo baya budadalika kukujayigwa.
