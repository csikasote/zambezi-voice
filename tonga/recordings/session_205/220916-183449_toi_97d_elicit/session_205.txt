##Utavuwi mufubafuba mbuli bufubafuba bwakwe, Kuti uteelani awe ayebo. Vuwa mufubafuba mbuli bufubafuba bwakwe,
##Kuti atasongwaali mumeso aakwe mwini. Uutuma makani kumulomo wamufubafuba Ulakosola zituta zyakwe, alimwi ulanywa lunyonyongano.
##Maulu aacilema alalengelela buyo; Mbukabede kasimpi mumilomo yabafubafuba. Mbuli muntu uuanga ibbwe munkwilipo,
##Mbwabede uupa mufubafuba bulemu, Mbuli bumvwa buyasa ijanza lyamukolwi, Mbukabede kasimpi mumilomo yabafubafuba,
##Mbuli simata uuyasa boonse buyo, Mbwabede muntu uubelesya mufubafuba ayooyo uubelesya mweendanzila.
##Mbuli mubwa uuboola kubulusi bwakwe, Mbwabede mufubafuba uuindulula bufubafuba bwakwe,
##Sa ulibwene muntu musongo mumeso aakwe mwini? Mufubafuba ulasyomeka kwiinda nguwe.
